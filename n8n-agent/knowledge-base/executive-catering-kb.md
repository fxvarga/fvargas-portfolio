## Company Overview

Executive Catering CT is Connecticut's premier full-service catering company specializing in corporate events, weddings, private parties, and community gatherings. Born from the culinary team behind 1 Stop Wings in Wallingford, CT, we bring bold flavors, scratch-made recipes, and professional event execution to every occasion.

We pride ourselves on flexible menus, hands-on service, and transparent pricing. Whether it's a 20-person board meeting or a 500-guest gala, every event receives the same attention to detail and culinary excellence.

## Catering Tiers & Pricing

### Budget Tier — $25–40 per person
Best for: Casual office lunches, team meetings, informal gatherings.

**Typical package includes:**
- Choice of 1 wing/tender flavor (50 pieces per 25 guests)
- 1 sandwich platter option
- 2 sides (half trays)
- Canned sodas and bottled water
- Paper plates, napkins, utensils (included)

### Standard Tier — $40–65 per person
Best for: Corporate luncheons, holiday parties, community events.

**Typical package includes:**
- Choice of 2–3 wing/tender flavors (mixed trays)
- 1–2 sandwich platter options
- Soup (seasonal, by the gallon)
- 2–3 sides (full trays)
- 1 salad tray
- Beverages: canned sodas, bottled water, iced tea or lemonade
- Disposable chafing dishes and serving utensils (included)

### Premium Tier — $65–100 per person
Best for: Executive events, milestone celebrations, plated or station service.

**Typical package includes:**
- Choice of 3–4 wing/tender flavors with dedicated sauce station
- Slow-roasted ribs (BBQ and/or Dry Rub)
- 2 sandwich platter options
- Soup course (both Classic and Creamy chicken)
- 3–4 sides (full trays)
- Full salad bar (Caesar, Italian, Spring Mix)
- Beverages: full beverage service
- Real chafing dishes, serving platters, tongs (provided and collected)
- On-site service staff (servers, setup/breakdown crew)

### Luxury Tier — $100+ per person
Best for: Weddings, galas, VIP corporate events.

**Typical package includes:**
- Full menu access: wings, tenders, ribs, sandwiches, salads, all sides
- Multiple flavor stations with live sauce tossing
- Dedicated carving/rib station
- Custom menu consultation
- Full beverage service including specialty drinks
- Premium serviceware (china-look disposables or real china upon request)
- Full event staff: servers, coordinator, chef on-site
- Setup, service, and complete breakdown/cleanup

## Terms & Policies

- **Deposit:** 50% of estimated total due at booking to secure date.
- **Final Payment:** Remaining balance due 5 business days before event.
- **Final Headcount:** Must be confirmed at least 7 days before event. After confirmation, headcount may increase but not decrease.
- **Cancellation Policy:**
  - 14+ days before event: Full refund minus 10% administrative fee.
  - 7–13 days before event: 50% refund.
  - Less than 7 days: No refund.
- **Proposal Validity:** All proposals are valid for 30 days from date of preparation.
- **Gratuity:** A 20% service charge is applied to Full Service and Luxury packages. This is distributed to the service team.
- **Dietary Accommodations:** We can accommodate most dietary needs (gluten-free, nut-free, vegetarian) with advance notice. Please specify at booking.
- **Minimum Order:** Catering minimum is 20 guests for drop-off, 40 guests for Full Service.

## Company Overview

OpsBlueprint is a workflow automation consulting firm that helps businesses eliminate manual, repetitive processes by designing and implementing custom automation solutions. We specialize in connecting disparate business tools, building intelligent workflows, and creating self-service operational systems that scale.

Our approach combines deep technical expertise in integration platforms (n8n, Make, Zapier, Power Automate) with business process analysis to deliver automations that genuinely reduce operational overhead, not just move complexity around.

We work with small-to-mid-sized businesses, startups, and departments within larger enterprises who are spending too much time on manual data entry, email routing, report generation, lead management, and cross-platform synchronization.

## Terms & Policies

- **Payment Terms:** 50% deposit at project kickoff, 50% upon completion. Managed services billed monthly in advance.
- **Cancellation:** Projects may be cancelled with 14 days' notice. Deposit is non-refundable after kickoff. Work completed to date will be delivered.
- **Intellectual Property:** All workflows, code, and documentation created during the engagement become the client's property upon final payment.
- **Confidentiality:** We sign NDAs upon request. All client data and processes are treated as confidential by default.
- **Proposal Validity:** All proposals are valid for 30 days from date of preparation.
- **Support SLA (Managed Services):**
  - Critical (workflow down): 4-hour response, 24-hour resolution target
  - High (degraded performance): 8-hour response, 48-hour resolution target
  - Normal (enhancement request): 24-hour response, scheduled in next sprint
- **Refund Policy:** If we cannot deliver the agreed-upon automation within the estimated timeline (excluding delays caused by client), we offer a 25% refund on the remaining balance.
